# PyCloud ☁️

## Get the latest features (Not Always True) here [Nakildias/PyCloud](https://pycloud.nakildias.com/Nakildias/PyCloud/tree/master/)

---

**PyCloud is a versatile, self-hostable application suite designed to integrate your digital life.** It offers a range of tools from productivity and development to entertainment and social connectivity. PyCloud aims to be your personal all-in-one platform, providing a seamless experience for managing files, code, notes, and communications, with exciting new social and AI capabilities.

---

## ✨ Key Features

PyCloud comes packed with a diverse set of features to enhance your productivity and digital interaction:

### Collaboration & Communication
* 🗣️ **General Group Chat:** Engage in real-time conversations with other users. You can even **share files directly within the chat**, and **admins have the power to delete any message**.
* 🤝 **Friends & Friend Chat:** Connect with friends, manage your friend list, and have **private conversations** with direct message support, including **private file sharing**.
* 🔔 **Notifications:** Stay updated with alerts for new followers, post and comment likes, new comments, shares, and even **collaborator invitations for Git repositories**.

### Productivity & Organization
* 🗄️ **File Storage:** Securely store and manage your files with **hierarchical folders**, **batch selection**, **renaming**, and **copy/paste capabilities**. Need to share something publicly? No problem – PyCloud supports **public sharing with optional password protection**.
* 📝 **Notes:** A dedicated space to jot down your thoughts, ideas, and important information. Create, view, and **edit notes directly in the browser**.
* ▶️ **YouTube Downloader:** Download videos and audio from YouTube directly to your cloud storage. Choose your preferred format and quality.
* 🖼️ **Image Upscaler:** Upscale images using basic resizing techniques, supporting **multiple common image formats** like JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP.
* 🖥️ **SSH Client:** Connect to your SSH servers directly through the Web.

### Development & Technical
* ⚙️ **Integrated Git Server:** Host and manage your **Git repositories** with web-based Browse of files, folders, and commit history. Supports both **private and public repositories**, **forks**, and **collaborator management**.
* 💻 **Code Editor with Syntax Highlighting:** Edit plain text files directly in the browser with ease, powered by [CodeMirror](https://codemirror.net/) for a rich coding experience with **syntax highlighting**.
* ⬆️ **File Uploads to Repositories:** Upload multiple files at once to any directory within your Git repositories.
* 🗑️ **Delete Repository Items:** Remove files and folders directly from your Git repositories through the web interface.
* 🤖 **AI Chat (Ollama Integration):** Interact with small or large AI models locally through Ollama, complete with **conversation history persistence**.

### User & Admin Management
* 👤 **User Profiles:** Personalize your PyCloud experience with a bio, a **custom profile picture (including animated GIFs!)**, and links to your social media (GitHub, Spotify, YouTube, X (Twitter), Steam, Twitch, Discord, and Reddit).
* 📧 **Changeable Email:** Users can update their registered email address.
* 🔑 **Password Reset via SMTP:** Securely reset forgotten passwords using email.
* 🛠️ **Admin Settings:** A comprehensive control panel for administrators to manage **global settings** like user registration, default storage limits, and maximum upload sizes.
* 🚫 **User Management:** Admins have granular control, able to **disable (ban) or permanently delete user accounts**, and manage **individual user storage and upload limits**.
* 🎨 **Customizable Themes:** Choose from various visual themes to personalize your PyCloud experience.
* ⭐ **Starred Repositories:** Keep track of your favorite Git repositories by starring them for quick access.

### Entertainment
* 🎮 **GBA Emulator:** Relive classic Game Boy Advance games directly in PyCloud.
    * Powered by [mGBA](https://github.com/mgba-emu/mgba), [Emscripten](https://github.com/emscripten-core/emscripten), and [GBA-wasm](https://github.com/kxkx5150/GBA-wasm).
